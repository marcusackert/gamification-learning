Level 21: Trial of Judgment

Write an if that sets status to "safe" if hp > 50.

Conditions let your code decide. Use if with a comparison to guard the hero’s health.

Level 22: Path of Decisions

Use if/else to set status to "safe" or "danger".

Sometimes there are two paths. Use else to handle the alternative outcome.

Level 23: The Many Doors

Use elif for three cases: danger, caution, safe.

More than two choices? elif lets you add middle conditions between if and else.

Level 24: Endless Corridor

Use a while loop to count to 3.

A while loop repeats until a condition is false. Imagine walking until you reach the exit.

Level 25: Avoid the Traps

Use continue to skip adding 3 when looping 1–5.

continue lets you skip a step in the loop. Like dodging a trap and moving forward.
